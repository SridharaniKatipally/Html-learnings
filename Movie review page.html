KGF CHAPTER-2

KGF Chapter 2 is a high-octane Indian action film that continues the story of Rocky, who rises from poverty to become the powerful ruler of the Kolar Gold Fields. As he consolidates his empire, Rocky faces intense opposition from political forces, crime lords, and a determined Prime Minister, Ramika Sen. With enemies closing in and his past catching up, Rocky must fight to protect his legacy and fulfill a promise to his mother. Directed by Prashanth Neel and starring Yash, the film blends intense action, emotional depth, and stylish storytelling to deliver a gripping cinematic experience.
